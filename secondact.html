The Playlist



The story starts off with a man who works as a coder at a small sweden auction website. One day, he decided to create a website named Advertigo.
Upon seeing a man named, Martin Lorentzon, he had requested for an appointment to sell his website named Advertigo for 10 million kronor.
While having a chat with Martin, he brings up the topic of competing with Silicon Valley.
Upon stumbling or finding the issues about Silicon Valley, Daniel Ek, had decided to create a new project.
A faster, free, and legal site for music to be played called Spotify.
He turned to Martin for assistance in completing the project.
Facing many ups and downs with legalizing music issues, changing people's opinion, and accomplishing the set goals.
He had finally found a chance to influence and let others know about his project to students who like listening to music.



The characters in the first episode of the series were very interesting. Daniel Ek was a person who had a brilliant mind and could create new original ideas.
Martin Lorentzon was a person who was a cheerful type of entrepreneur and open to business ideas once persuaded.
Andreas Ehn was a person who looked responsible and a hard worker.
The singer, Bobbie, seemed like a great singer and influence on the spotify project considering the fact that she is a singer whose gigs are illegally pirated in Silicon Valley.



The series, “The Playlist”, connects with the hardship of most entrepreneurs having trouble having a chance on introducing their own projects and ideas.
It is hard for small web developers to show their work due to other bigger web developers grabbing attention.
Therefore, some lose faith or hope in showing their work to the world.
As an entrepreneur the project they are working on must be catchy to people and profitable, which most of them have troubles with.
There are various projects developers had created but were not profitable or catchy enough which would require them to exert more money to waste or work harder for people.
It was saddening, knowing that most people who pursued business suffer from such loss and hard work.
But in the end, the path they take will determine the future of their project.
